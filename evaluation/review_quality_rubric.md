# Review Quality Rubric

Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 1 | Poor or missing |
| 2 | Weak |
| 3 | Acceptable |
| 4 | Good |
| 5 | Excellent |

## Dimensions

### 1. Factual Accuracy

The review matches the actual logs and does not invent facts.

### 2. Goal Relevance

The review connects findings back to stated goals and projects.

### 3. Positive Recognition

The review notices completed work, maintenance work, recovery, or small wins.

### 4. Actionability

The next steps are specific enough for the user to act on.

### 5. Restraint

The review does not overload the user with too many suggestions.

### 6. Slack Protection

The review protects buffer time and avoids filling every available hour.

### 7. Risk Detection

The review identifies drift, dormancy, overload, or draining patterns without blaming the user.

## External Feedback Questions

Ask 2-3 classmates:

1. Was the review understandable?
2. Did the review feel encouraging rather than discouraging?
3. Did the next-week suggestion feel realistic?
4. Did the review help you understand goal progress?
5. Did the review make the week feel more controllable?
